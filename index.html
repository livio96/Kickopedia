About Us

Our Mission

The consumer mindset balance is shifting significantly towards e-commerce. The opportunities for small & medium resellers to discover and flip products online are growing at a record speed. Kickopedia’s mission is to make your life easier in finding such opportunities on a daily basis. For now, we have focused our work on sneakers

We help you achieve this by following 3 trivial steps

  • Discover a potential product
  • Analyze the product
  • Flip it online
